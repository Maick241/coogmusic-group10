Team 10 Checkpoint #1
Online Music Library Mini-world & Requirements

Entities:
-	User
-	Song/Track
-	Album
-	Playlist
-	Artist
-	History 
-	Likes
-	Genre
-	Rating

Attributes:
User:
-	UserID (Int, Primary Key)
-	Username (Varchar(30), Not Null, Unique, No special characters)
-	UserPassword (Varchar(20), Not Null)
-	FirstName (Varchar(30), Not Null, No special characters)
-	LastName (Varchar(30), Not Null, No special characters)
-	DateOfBirth (Date, Not Null)
-	Email (Varchar(255), Unique, No special characters besides “@” & “.”, Not Null)
-	UserType (Listener, Administrator, Artist, Developer)
-	DateJoined (Date, Not Null)
-	Country (Varchar(30), Not Null)
-	City (Varchar(30))
-	IsOnline (Boolean)
-	AccountStatus (Active, Suspended, or Banned)

Artist:
-	ArtistID (Int, Primary/Foreign Key)
-	ArtistBio (Varchar(275), No special characters)
-	ArtistPFP (BLOB)
-	VerifiedStatus (Boolean[True = Verified, False = Not Verified])
-	VerifyingAdminID (Int, Foreign Key, Not Null)
-	DateVerified (Timestamp, Not Null)

Album:
-	AlbumID (Int, Primary Key)
-	ArtistID (Int, Foreign Key)
-	AlbumName (Varchar(30), Not Null, No special characters)
-	AlbumArt (BLOB)
-	AlbumDate (Timestamp, Not Null)
-	AlbumType (Single, EP, or Album)
-	AlbumDescription (Varchar(275))

Song/Track:
-	SongID (Int, Primary Key)
-	SongLength (Int[Seconds], >0 & <3600)
-	SongName (Varchar(50), No special characters)
-	GenreID (Int, Foreign Key)
-	AlbumID (Int, Foreign key)
-	Listens (Int, Default = 0)
-	SongFile (BLOB, Unique)
-	FileFormat (Varchar(10))
-	FileSize (BigInt)
-	Explicit (Boolean [True = Explicit, False = Not Explicit])
-	TrackNumber (Int, Not Null, Unique, Ordered)
-	CopyRightInfo (Varchar(275))

Playlist:
-	PlaylistID (Int, Primary Key)
-	UserID (Int, Foreign Key, Not Null)
-	PlaylistName (Varchar(50), Default = [Playlist1,Playlist2,...])
-	PlaylistDescription (Varchar(275))
-	PublicPlaylist (Boolean [True = Public, False = Private])
-	PlaylistImage (BLOB)
-	DateCreated (Timestamp, Not Null)
-	Shuffle (Boolean [True = Shuffles songs, False = Plays songs sequentially], Default = False)

Genre:
-	GenreID (Int, Primary Key)
-	GenreName (Varchar(30), Not Null, Unique)
-	GenreDescription (Varchar(275))

History:
-	HistoryID (Int, Primary Key)
-	UserID (Int, Foreign Key)

Likes:
-	LikesID (Int, Primary Key)
-	UserID (Int, Foreign Key, Not Null)

Rating:
-	RatingID (Int, Primary Key)
-	UserID (Int, Foreign Key)
-	RatingValue (Decimal(2,1), >=0.0 & <=5.0)
-	RatingDate (TimeStamp, Not Null)
-	RatingDescription (Varchar(275))
-	RateType (Song, Playlist, Album, Artist)
-	RatedTypeID (Int, Foreign Key, Not Null)

Associative Entities:
UserFollows:
-	FollowerID (Int, Primary/Foreign Key)
-	ArtistID (Int, Primary/Foreign Key)
-	DateFollowed (Date, Not Null)

LikedSong:
-	LikesID (Int, Primary/Foreign Key)
-	SongID (Int, Primary/Foreign Key)
-	TimeLiked (Timestamp, Not Null)
-	LikedPosition (Int, Ordered by least recently listened to)

PlaylistSong:
-	PlaylistID (Primary/Foreign Key)
-	SongID (Primary/Foreign Key)
-	TimeAdded (Timestamp, Not Null)
-	PlaylistPosition (Int, Unique)

HistorySong:
-	SongID (Int, Primary/Foreign Key)
-	HistoryID (Int Primary/Foreign Key)
-	TimeListened (Timestamp, Not Null)
-	HistoryPosition (Int, Increment)
Relationships:
-	User hasPlaylist Playlist (0:N)
-	User isArtist Artist (0:1)
-	User hasLikes Likes (1:1)
-	User hasHistory History (1:1)
-	Album hasRating Rating (0:N)
-	Song hasRating Rating (1:N)
-	Artist hasRating Rating (1:N)
-	Album hasSong Song/Track (1:N)
-	Artist hasAlbum Album (1:N)
-	Song/Track songGenre Genre (1:N)
-	Album albumGenre Genre (1:N)
-	Artist artistGenre Genre (1:N)
-	Artist hasAlbum Album (1:N)
